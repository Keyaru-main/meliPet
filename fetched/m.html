Adding Terraria Content to Minecraft

Description

 

DISCORD

 

 

This mod adds content from Terraria.

The plan is to add 95+% of all Terraria content to Minecraft. Our team is working on it.

 

DOWNLOAD LATEST VERSIONS ONLY THROUGH OUR LAUNCHER HERE! Includes newest versions alongside early access builds.

Disclaimer:

  • This mod is a standalone project that is NOT intended to be used as a part of a modpack. Only addons are supported.
  • OptiFine? NO. Unless cubic chunks will support it in the future.
  • This mod is NOT supported for android/ios emulated Minecraft: Java  Edition, do not ask for support.
  • This mod has troubles working on dedicated servers and generally is NOT intended to work with it.

IF YOU ARE A YOUTUBER WILLING TO RECORD A SHOWCASE (10k+ subscribers), ASK US ON OUR DISCORD TO PROVIDE YOU EARLY ACCESS

 

NOTE:

  • *This mod requires ** * MusicLayer 12.2.22+* *to work!
  • *This mod requires ** * HammerLib 12.2.49+* *to work!
  • *This mod requires ** * HammerAnimations 12.2.21+* *to work!
  • This mod requires*   OpenCubicChunks 0.0.1055+ *to work!
  • *This mod is compatible with ** * Corsair McCUE 12.4.10!
  • *This mod looks a lot better with ** * ColoredLux 12.6.33+* *to work!
  • Configs are located at .minecraft/terraria/ModConfigs.cfg

Some images:

 

If you want to get content earlier, feel free to subscribe to our Patreon!

You also need a paid Minecraft account, and you have to be on our Terraria Craft discord server.

 

**Permissions:**

  • You can use this mod in your modpack.
  • You are not allowed to re-publish this mod to other sites without providing a CurseForge download link and my permission (PM to   Zeitheron, or via Discord)
  • You are not allowed to include this (or any other mods of the DragonForge team) in your own mod. Building add-ons, however, is allowed, but a friendly PM would be good.

Note:

  • The only supported version of Minecraft is the latest one for which this mod exists. DO NOT ASK TO UPGRADE OR DOWNGRADE!
